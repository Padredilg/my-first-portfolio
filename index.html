About Me

My name is Luiz Guilherme Padredi. I am a 24 years old Aerospace Engineering student at University of Central Florida, and I am also currently learning coding languages with UCF's Coding Bootcamp program. From the moment I started college in 2019 to now, I have acquired my AA degree, and I am more than halfway through my path to acquiring my Bachelor's Degree. I have also discovered that I really enjoy coding which is why I have started it now, in hopes that not only will I become more marketable, but also will land a job in which I can apply all of my skills and call it my dream job.
